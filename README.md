# 📊 Sales Performance & Logistics Dashboard (My First Power BI Project!)

## 🎯 Project Overview
Welcome to my very first Power BI project! This dashboard was built to explore, clean, and visualize retail sales data. The core objective was to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions—such as customer segments, geographical regions, and logistics (shipping modes)—transforming raw operational data into an interactive visual story.

This project served as a foundational milestone for me to master data cleaning, modeling, and dashboard design principles.

---

## 🚀 Key Areas of Analysis & Insights

* **📊 Monthly Sales Trends:** Tracking revenue fluctuations over time to identify seasonal spikes and historical growth patterns.
* **🚚 Shipping Mode Efficiency:** Visualizing sales distribution by ship mode to understand customer logistics preferences.
* **💼 Customer Segment Performance:** Breaking down sales across different sectors (e.g., Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) to find the primary revenue drivers.
* **📈 Regional Profit Comparison:** Evaluating profitability across different territories to pinpoint high-margin zones vs. underperforming markets.
* **💡 Quantity Ordered by Region:** Analyzing product volume demand geographically to support inventory and supply chain awareness.

---

## 🛠️ Skills & Technical Techniques Applied

Through building this dashboard from scratch, I successfully developed and practiced the following BI skills:
* **Data Cleansing & Staging:** Importing raw data into **Power Query**, handling missing values, fixing data types, and ensuring the dataset was structured correctly.
* **Interactive Filtering:** Implementing synchronized filters and slicers to allow users to dynamically explore data by segment or region.
* **Visual Hierarchy & Design:** Choosing a consistent color palette and clean layout utilizing **Bar Charts, Line Charts, and Pie/Donut Visuals** to make the metrics easily scannable for any viewer.
